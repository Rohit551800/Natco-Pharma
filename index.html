ABOUT THE DIVISION

Built on patent knowledge. Pointed at the field.

NATCO's Crop Health Sciences division grew out of the company's pharmaceutical chemistry — the same techno-legal capability that built our drug portfolio now builds crop protection. It shows up first in our Chlorantraniliprole-based line, sold across India's major agricultural belts.

It shows up again in our pheromone-based mating disruption product — India's first — built to manage pink bollworm in cotton without leaning harder on chemical load.

” Every new formulation is screened against one question: can green chemistry get us the same result with less hazard? That bias shapes our R&D pipeline, not just our marketing.
